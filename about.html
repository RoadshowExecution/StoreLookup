How to use the Roadshow Host Venues Tool:

• Enter your store's Street Address into the "Enter your Store's Street Address" Search Field

• Press Enter -or- Click the Blue Magnifying Glass Button

• If no results are found, change the Within drop-down to 120 miles

- If no results are found within 90 miles, please get DHRM Approval before registering your associates -

• Select the desired Available Venue & Date

• Highlight and Copy the Offering ID

• Navigate to Knowledge Depot

• Click the myAdmin tab

• Click the Register Associate for a Course link

• Click "Next" on the orders screen

• Check the "Show exact matches only" box

• Paste the Offering ID into the Search bar
